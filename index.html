My process

My design process based on best practices in user experience design and adapted for my clients. These 3 product design levels help me create best user expirience and don’t miss anything important

Define

This level contains basic information about the product. What problems it solves and how, who the users are, what the business goals. This is the core of the product, its uniqueness, what determines its value

Prototype

At this level, the basic information is transformed into interface elements, screens, navigation, information delivery. This level determines the convenience of working with the product

Deliver

This is level of visual and emotional perception of the product. It’s contains final interactive mockup of all product for all screens, which users see and with whom they interact
